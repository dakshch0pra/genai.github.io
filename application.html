Application of Generative AI

In this section, we will learn about use-cases and applications of Generative AI everywhere today.

Use-Cases of Generative AI

    Generative AI has evolved far beyond its initial chatbot applications, now driving innovation across various fields. From creating stunning visuals and compelling stories to revolutionizing business strategies and educational tools, this technology is reshaping the way we interact with digital content. Let's dive deeper into the use-cases of Generative AI:

  • Creative Arts

    Generative AI aids artists in composing music, writing scripts, and editing content. It enhances videos with effects, animations, and storytelling, and can seamlessly edit images. Beyond creation, AI assists in brainstorming and refining ideas. These are included in wordsmithing tasks discussed below.

  • Gaming Industry

    Generative AI designs detailed characters and dynamic worlds, enhancing gaming with lifelike visuals and interactive experiences. It creates real-time, personalized gameplay based on player choices, transforming how games are developed and experienced.

  • Business and Marketing

    AI simplifies product design by analyzing market trends and aids marketing with content creation, from engaging posts to visuals. It also powers chatbots for personalized customer support, reducing costs while improving user experience.

  • Research and Development

    AI accelerates research by analyzing large datasets, summarizing papers, and predicting outcomes. It enables breakthroughs by simulating findings, driving innovations from drug discovery to aerospace. These are included in wordsmithing tasks discussed below.

  • Education and Training

    Generative AI revolutionizes education with interactive learning materials and personalized tutoring. It simplifies complex topics and adapts to individual learning needs, making education more efficient and engaging.

How to use Generative AI

Generative AI tools are trained on masses of data, and they create models based on these data. When you give an AI tool a prompt, it uses the data model to generate a response.

We can use CLEAR framework to remember how to generate good prompts in text-based daily basis tools such as ChatGPT.


  • Clear

    Be specific, Use simple language and, Prioritize critical information
    Example:
    “Translate text to French.”

  • Logical

    Structure info in order (logical flow), Establish context and relationships and, Avoid too many instructions in a single prompt
    Example:
    “Are there any fundamental principles or laws for this branch of physics?”

  • Explicit

    Define instructions, Set reading levels and output formats and Assign a role for ChatGPT to play
    Example:
    “You are an undergraduate science student. Describe the Krebs cycle in simple terms.”


  • Adaptive

    Rephrase, Restructure, Try different approaches, Be creative with prompts
    Example:
    “Could you provide more examples?”

  • Reflective

    Carefully evaluate AI responses, Identify areas for improvement (it takes time) and Use insights to further refine strategies for engagement
    TRUST BUT VERIFY!
    Example:
    Can I verify the information using other sources?
